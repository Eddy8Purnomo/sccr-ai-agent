## About
SCCR (Stem Cell and Cancer Research) Indonesia is an independent institution focused on pharmaceutical and biomedical research. Founded on June 7, 2012 by Prof. Dr. dr. Agung Putra, M.Si.Med.

## Vision
"Strongest in Science, Leading in Technology, and Precision in Results."  
Aims to become a world-class research institution with aspirations toward global scientific recognition.

## Scope
- Regenerative medicine (stem cells)
- Cancer research
- Personalized & Precision Medicine
- Biotechnology development

## Leadership
- **Founder**: Prof. Dr. dr. Agung Putra, M.Si.Med. (Professor, Sultan Agung University)
- **CEO**: Ninuk Indirawaty Deversiani

## Key Products
- **Stem Cells**: From umbilical cord (99.8% purity)
- **Secretome**: Anti-inflammatory, tissue-regenerative factors
- **CTL Therapy**: Cytotoxic T-Lymphocyte for targeted cancer treatment
